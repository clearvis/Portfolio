My Bio

Freelance web designer and developer, owner of ProWeb Innovations, with a passion for User Interface Design. Love coding to make designs come to life.

When I´m away from the keyboard you can find me blowing bubbles underwater with my friends at The Scuba Sports Club, chasing a ball around on a racquetball court, and in the summer, playing a little softball. When it´s time to relax there´s always room for a little dark beer, or nice red wine.

Scuba and Underwater Photography

My wife and I have been scuba diving since 1998, traveling to tropical destinations whenever we get the chance. Not only the opportunities to see the world as so few do underwater, but the experience of meeting new people and their different cultures, has made me realize that there´s more to life than working to make money. Happiness can be found all around us in the friends we have and the acquaintances we meet. I have tried to document my scuba diving vacations with videos and photographs, that I will keep updated here and across my social networks.
